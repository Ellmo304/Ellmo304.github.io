About Me

I am a mixed media fine art graduate with a genuine passion for creativity and design. Art and music have always been an integral part of my life. Upon leaving university, I spent several years working in horticulture and soft landscaping where I developed a diligent work ethic, an eye for detail and an understanding of meeting client’s expectations. After leaving the world of horticulture for a fresh challenge, I spent last year working in a primary school where I learned to work cohesively as part of much larger team to achieve staff wide targets. It was extremely rewarding helping to educate the younger generation and it was here I was also introduced to coding, as it’s now part of the national curriculum. A thirst to constantly learn and take on new challenges has now driven me to pursue a career in web development where I believe I can combine my love for technology with my creative qualities, and other aforementioned skills and utilise these abilities in building a successful career for myself.
