About Me

I'm Elliot, a full stack web developer based in London. From as young as I can remember, I've had a genuine passion for creativity and design. Whether it was drawing pictures of Batman when I was three years old or receiving my Bachelors Degree in Mixed Media Fine Art at University, painting, designing and making music have always been an integral part of my life and this is predominantly the way I choose to spend my free time. Upon leaving university, I spent several years working in horticulture and soft landscaping where I developed a diligent work ethic, an eye for detail and an understanding of meeting clientâ€™s expectations, as well as discovering a new found interest and affection for horticulture.

Now, a thirst to constantly learn and take on new challenges has driven me to pursue a career in web development where I hope to combine and utilize my love of technology, creative strengths, and previous job experience into building a successful career as a developer. Having freshly graduated from General Assembly London, where I developed a strong foundation in JavaScript programming, I am keen to keep coding and already have two new projects underway. The first is a website for a newly qualified personal trainer and fitness instructor hoping to gain new clients and the second is an online Art community where users can share reviews, images and more from the latest exhibitions they've been to. I aim to expand on my current skill base by learning ReactJS this year in addition to further building on the languages and frameworks I have worked with so far.
